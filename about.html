About Workspace

A lightweight personal workspace for students.

Workspace is a browser-based productivity environment that keeps the things students juggle — class notes, to-do lists, a calendar, homework reminders, and group chat — together in a single tab. It runs entirely in the browser, requires no install, and works on any modern device including Chromebooks.

What's included

  • Notes — keep per-class notes with quick search.
  • Planner — simple to-do list with reminders.
  • Group chat — message classmates for study help.
  • Reading list — save articles and videos for later.
  • Music — lightweight background listening.

Privacy

Workspace stores your notes in your own account. Passwords are never stored in plain text. We do not sell user data. We do not show ads.

For educators

Workspace is suitable for classroom environments. It does not host inappropriate content, gambling, adult material, or age-restricted media.
