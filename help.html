Workspace — Help

Getting started

Do I need an account?

You can try the workspace without one, but notes and lists won't sync. Signing in enables syncing across devices.

Does it work on Chromebooks?

Yes. Workspace runs in any modern browser, including Chrome on Chromebooks. No install is required.

How do I reset my password?

Use the "Forgot?" link on the login page. A reset code will be sent to your registered email or chat account.

Notes & planner

Where are my notes stored?

Notes are stored per-user on our servers and cached in your browser. You own your content and can export it at any time.

Is there a limit on notes?

Free accounts can store up to several hundred notes. There's no file-size limit for text, but attachments are capped at 10 MB.

Privacy

Do you sell my data?

No. We do not sell user data to third parties, and we do not serve advertising. We use cookies only for logging you in.

Is this safe for school use?

Yes. Workspace does not host adult content, gambling, or age-restricted material. It is intended as a study aid.
